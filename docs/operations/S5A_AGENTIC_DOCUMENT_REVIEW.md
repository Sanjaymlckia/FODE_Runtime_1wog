# S5A Agentic Document Review

Date: 2026-05-09
Scope: AI-assisted review philosophy

## Allowed AI Review Actions

- Flag missing fields or files.
- Score document readability or completeness.
- Classify probable document quality issues.
- Summarize intake gaps for an operator.
- Detect likely duplicates or suspicious inconsistencies.

## Forbidden AI Review Actions

- Finalize admission.
- Finalize rejection.
- Finalize payment verification.
- Override Admin authority.
- Send unattended outbound communication.
- Mutate sheet rows or Drive contents.

## Review Philosophy

- AI review is advisory only.
- AI can reduce operator load by surfacing likely issues.
- Human review remains the authority for final decisions.
- Review outputs should be explainable and auditable.

## Practical Guidance

- Treat AI scores as triage hints, not decisions.
- Use AI to prioritize review queues.
- Keep review criteria stable and documented.
- Preserve operator ability to disagree with AI findings.
